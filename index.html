Department of External Affairs

The Department of External Affairs (DoEA) serves as the Foundation's primary interface with civilian, governmental, and corporate entities outside the Foundation's direct control. Tasked with maintaining the veil of normalcy, the DoEA operates through diplomatic channels, misinformation campaigns, and strategic alliances.
